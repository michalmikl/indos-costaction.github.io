title: Working Groups
---

The action is structured into several working groups, each focusing on specific research and coordination activities. These working groups ensure comprehensive coverage of our research objectives and facilitate effective collaboration. Each working group has a leader, one or multiple co-leaders, and can consist of multiple task forces.

- [WG1: Quality Assessment and Quality Control](/wg1)
- [WG2: Metadata](/wg2)
- [WG3: Automated Preprocessing Pipelines](/wg3)
- [WG4: Ethics, Practices, and Regulations](/wg4)
- [WG5: Dissemination and Communication](/wg5)
