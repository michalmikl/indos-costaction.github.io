---
title: Join the INDoS network
---

We are always looking for collaboration opportunities with:

- **Researchers:** Join our research activities and contribute to publications
- **Industry Partners:** Apply our methodologies to real-world problems
- **Students:** Participate in training schools and Short-Term Scientific Missions (STSMs)
- **Funding Agencies:** Support our research and networking activities

If you are interested in joining as a member or participant, you can sign up for an [e-COST account](https://e-services.cost.eu/) and join one of the [working groups](https://www.cost.eu/actions/CA24161/#tabs+Name:Working%20Groups%20and%20Membership) on the COST website. As soon as your request to join has been processed, you will be automatically added to the mailing lists.

If you have questions, please contact one of the the [working group](/working-groups) coordinators.
